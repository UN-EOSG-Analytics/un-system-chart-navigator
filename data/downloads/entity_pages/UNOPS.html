UNOPS provides practical solutions in infrastructure, procurement and project management. 

Together with our partners, we support peace and security, humanitarian and development projects around the world – addressing urgent needs and helping shape a more sustainable, resilient and inclusive future.

Project highlights

LEARN MORE
  • Afghanistan

    Amid intensifying needs, a World Bank-financed project is providing a lifeline for vulnerable households.
  • Ukraine

    Together with the UN family, the government of Ukraine, and other partners, we provide vital support to communities impacted by the ongoing war.
  • Argentina

    In northern Argentina, indigenous communities are more resilient to droughts thanks to improved access to clean water.
  • Gaza

    With partners, we’re supporting critical humanitarian efforts and the delivery of life-saving aid in Gaza.
  • Nepal

    In Nepal, UNOPS, Cities Alliance and UN Habitat are helping design gender responsive and climate-resilient public spaces.
  • Yemen

    In Yemen, rural and urban recovery projects are helping restore critical services damaged by years of conflict and extreme weather.
  • Global

    From Afghanistan to Mozambique, we work with the UN family and partners to respond to critical needs and support vulnerable communities.
